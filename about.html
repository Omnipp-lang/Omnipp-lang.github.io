About Omni++

Omni++ is a powerful new programming language designed as the spiritual successor to C++. Built for developers who demand performance, flexibility, and future-proofing, Omni++ merges low-level power with high-level features.

It supports both AOT (Ahead-of-Time) and JIT (Just-in-Time) compilation, and includes built-in compatibility with:

  • Over 40+ modern C++ supersets and extensions
  • Web3 smart contract development via the integrated Crypted engine
  • Cross-language interoperability with C, C++, C#, JavaScript, and Python

Omni++ is ideal for:

  • System and embedded developers
  • AI and scientific researchers
  • Game engine creators and graphics programmers
  • Blockchain and Web3 builders
  • Anyone looking to escape the limitations of traditional C++

With a developer-first philosophy, modern toolchain, and an open ecosystem (minus the compiler backend), Omni++ is engineered for speed, control, and unlimited extensibility.
